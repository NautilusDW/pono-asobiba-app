# Prompts

## Stage 1 Scrolling Background

Create a wide horizontal scrolling Stage 1 background panorama for a children's educational underwater album game. Scene: tide pool and shallow shore, bright clear turquoise water, sandy bottom, rocks, seaweed, shells, small corals, gentle sunbeams from the water surface. Extra-wide 3:1 horizontal panorama, side-scrolling game background, seamless-feeling left-to-right exploration with visual landmarks across the width. No characters, no creatures, no submarine, no text, no UI. Polished Japanese children's picture-book game style, soft watercolor texture, clean readable shapes, high detail but not cluttered. Keep the horizon and water surface consistent across the full width.

## Stage 1 Creature Expression Sheet

Create one raw expression asset sheet for Stage 1 creatures only, on a pure white background. 6 rows x 3 columns grid with generous white spacing and faint crop-safe separation. Rows: hermit crab, small shrimp, sea star, tidepool goby, sea anemone, horseshoe crab boss. Columns: normal, eating food orb, happy after eating. Use the same camera angle, scale, colors, and design for each creature across the three expressions. Cute polished Japanese children's educational game style, watercolor-and-clean-sprite look, rounded friendly shapes, readable at small size. Do not include labels, text, UI, background scenery, shadows crossing panels, or a submarine. Raw image only; no alpha channel and no crop.
